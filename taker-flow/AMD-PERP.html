AMD-PERP — taker buy-side ratio distributions

Per time bucket: ratio = Σ(long taker $) / Σ|all taker $| ∈ [0,1] (notional = |quote+fee|); 0.5 = balanced, >0.5 = takers net buying. Rows = bucket size (1 min / 10 min / 1 hour); columns = all time / day (RTH 13:30-20:00 UTC) / night (non-RTH). Empty buckets excluded; y = probability density. At 1-min most buckets have all-same-direction flow, so the ratio piles up at 0 and 1.
