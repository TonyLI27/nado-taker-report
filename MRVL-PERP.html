3 · Hedge effect (real underlying via Yahoo 1m)

At open (13:30), noon (16:00) and close (~19:59) UTC each trading day we trade the underlying at the Yahoo price to flatten our Nado net position; hedge held between rebalances; cost 5bps of traded notional. Both value curves reset to 0 at the window start. Yahoo 1m only covers ~the last 30 days.

4 · Hedge via Hyperliquid HIP-3 (xyz MRVL) — 10-min rebalance

Hedge in the matching Hyperliquid xyz perp, dollar-neutral, rebalanced every 10 min over the full overlap (HL data 2026-02→06, 24/7). cost 5bps; the hedge leg is a perp so it pays HL funding (long pays when rate>0). hedged_value = unhedged + price-hedge P&L + HL funding P&L − cost. Positions shown in notional USD (SPY→SP500 10×, QQQ→XYZ100 40× scale handled by dollar-neutral sizing).

5 · Taker buy-side ratio distributions

For each time bucket: ratio = Σ(long taker $) / Σ|all taker $| ∈ [0,1] (notional = |quote+fee|); 0.5 = balanced, >0.5 = takers net buying. Rows = bucket size (1 min / 10 min / 1 hour); columns = all time / day (RTH) / night (non-RTH). Empty buckets excluded; y = probability density. Note: at 1-min most buckets have all-same-direction flow so the ratio piles up at 0 and 1.
